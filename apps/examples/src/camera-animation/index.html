Camera Animation

Demonstrates smooth animated camera transitions using @ue-too/animate. Click anywhere on the canvas and the camera will smoothly pan to that world position over 1 second.

  • Click anywhere to animate the camera to that position
  • Scroll to zoom, Middle Mouse Drag or Space + Left Drag to pan manually
  • Trackpad: Two-finger drag to pan, Pinch to zoom
  • Touch: Two-finger drag to pan, Pinch to zoom
  • A ruler overlay shows the current viewport boundaries
