Ruler Example

Displays a measurement ruler overlay along the edges of the canvas. The ruler ticks update dynamically as you pan and zoom, showing world-space coordinates.

  • Scroll to zoom — watch the ruler scale adapt
  • Middle Mouse Drag or Space + Left Drag to pan — the ruler numbers shift to reflect the visible region
  • Trackpad: Two-finger drag to pan, Pinch to zoom
  • Touch: Two-finger drag to pan, Pinch to zoom
