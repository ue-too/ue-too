Image Example

Upload an image to draw it on the pannable and zoomable canvas. Colored axis arrows (red = Y, green = X) at the origin show the world coordinate directions.

  • Click Choose Image to upload a file
  • Scroll to zoom in on the image
  • Middle Mouse Drag or Space + Left Drag to pan around
  • Trackpad: Two-finger drag to pan, Pinch to zoom
  • Touch: Two-finger drag to pan, Pinch to zoom
