Base Example

The simplest setup: a Board attached to a canvas with built-in pan, zoom, and rotate.

  • Scroll to zoom in and out
  • Middle Mouse Drag or Space + Left Drag to pan the viewport
  • Trackpad: Two-finger drag to pan, Pinch to zoom
  • Touch: Two-finger drag to pan, Pinch to zoom
  • Click on the canvas to log the world coordinates to the console
